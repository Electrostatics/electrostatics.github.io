There is no need to neutralize the simulation domain for Poisson-Boltzmann equations.  The version of the PB equation that APBS solves is generated from the grand canonical ensemble which means that ions are able to move into the simulation domain based on the charge of the solute and the chemical potential (ionic strength) of the bath.

I hope this helps,

Nathan
