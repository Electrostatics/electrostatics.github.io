Hi Lizhen!

 

Because of the way that PBD2PQR works and the ambiguity of the residue names A, C, and G we require that you use residue names RA, RC, RG, and RU for RNA and DT, DA, DC, and DG for DNA. Before we made this change we assumed you wanted RNA when you might have meant DNA and would “fix” the residue accordingly.
